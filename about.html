ABOUT JESS

Jess is a quirky animation artist and illustrator from the UK, with a passion for colour and storytelling.

She graduated from the Arts University Bournemouth in 2018 with the award-winning short film 'Whispering Onion', and has since produced artwork for Karrot Animation, Wickes, and Frosts among other clients. Frosts' 'Elf Academy' short, on which Jess worked as a background artist, has over 22,000 views to date.
